<objective>
Stand up the discovery + centralized-config infra pair so every later Spring Boot module in the project can register and fetch its config from a known address. ARCH-02 (Eureka discovery), ARCH-03 (Spring Cloud Config Server centralizes per-service config), and ARCH-11 (cold-boot resilience) all anchor on the two services produced by this plan.

Purpose:
- **Eureka server** at `http://eureka-server:8761/eureka/` is the runtime registry the api-gateway (01-06) and every business service (Phase 3+) registers into. Without it, the gateway's discovery-locator (D-14) has nothing to discover.
- **Config server** at `http://config-server:8888` serves the centralized `application.yml` baseline that every Boot app consumes via `spring.config.import` (Cross-Cutting #2 / RESEARCH §4.2). Without it, downstream services can't pull their Eureka URL, datasource template, or Springdoc keys without duplicating them everywhere.
- The **shared** `config-server/src/main/resources/config/application.yml` shipped here is the single source of truth that every business service inherits (CD-05). Per-service `<svc>-service.yml` and the `service-template.yml` baseline are owned by 01-06 and 01-07 — this plan must NOT create them.

Output:
- Two runnable Spring Boot modules (`eureka-server`, `config-server`) replacing the `.gitkeep` stubs from 01-01.
- Shared config-repo baseline `config-server/src/main/resources/config/application.yml`.
- `docker-compose.yml` extended (additively) with both services, both healthchecked.
</objective>

# All 13 business services share the same Postgres host (D-01..D-04 — single DB `n11`,

# Per-service `<svc>-service.yml` injects `db.user` / `db.password` / `flyway.schema`.
spring:
  datasource:
    url: jdbc:postgresql://${POSTGRES_HOST:postgres}:${POSTGRES_PORT:5432}/${POSTGRES_DB:n11}
    username: ${db.user:CHANGE-ME-IN-PER-SERVICE-YAML}
    password: ${db.password:CHANGE-ME-IN-PER-SERVICE-YAML}
    driver-class-name: org.postgresql.Driver
    hikari:
      maximum-pool-size: 10
      minimum-idle: 2
      connection-timeout: 5000

    open-in-view: false
    properties:
      hibernate:
